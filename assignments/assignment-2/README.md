# Assignment 2: Texturing, Animation, and Interaction in a 3D Campus Scene

This assignment extends my campus model from Assignment 1 by adding textures, animations, and user interaction.

## New Additions

- **Textures**: Added brick textures to buildings and grass/pavement textures for the ground
- **Glass Material**: IT Support building now has a transparent glass look
- **External Models**: Loaded benches and trees as GLTF models, plus animated people (FBX)
- **Animation**: Point light orbits around the roundabout, people have talking animations
- **Interactions**: Click buildings to change color, hover to highlight, press 'L' to toggle light

## Controls

- Mouse drag to rotate camera
- Scroll to zoom
- Click on buildings to change their color
- Press L to turn the point light on/off
